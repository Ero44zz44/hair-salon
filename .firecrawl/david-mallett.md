Boost your volume

**Here are four tips to thicken fine hair:**

**Trim your hair regularly.**

By cutting the ends regularly, hair appears visibly fuller **.**

**Take care of your scalp.**

Apply our Australian Salt Scrub shampoo once a week. This exclusive exfoliating shampoo has been formulated to purify, detoxify, and revitalize your hair from root to tip. Made with purified pink salt from the Murray River—a rare and precious ingredient—and white clay, **this natural scrub gently removes impurities and residue while revitalizing the scalp.**

**Use essential oils at night.**

An essential oil treatment can help stimulate hair growth. **The Elixir, a luxurious 100% natural regenerating oil**, is based on a complex combining the benefits of vitamin E with the virtues of seven precious plant oils. It nourishes, repairs, and targets damaged areas of the hair and scalp. **A vegan treatment, free from silicones and sulfates.**

**Carry a valuable ally in your bag: Volume Powder.**

An innovative texturizing spray that provides instant volume and density, helping to revive even the finest hair. Hair appears thicker, fuller, and perfectly styled, with lifted roots and a matte finish.

A new New York setting

David Mallett continues his expansion in New York with the opening of a second salon in the city. Located in the WSA, an iconic building in Manhattan, this new space blends Parisian elegance with New York modernity. Captivated by “this building with a 1980s look, like Talking Heads,” Mallett recounts having an immediate crush on the place perched on the 23rd floor.

The decor, sleek and bright, combines noble materials, neutral tones, and touches of greenery, and is in line with the refined and minimalist aesthetic dear to the Brand. The building, which has become a hotspot for fashion and well-being,already brings together prestigious creators and brands as well as the Water Lounge center. After its two Parisian salons and a first New York salon, this new address confirms the growing international presence of David Mallett and his influence on the global beauty scene.
